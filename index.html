Amicis Cor Musica, Inc

Inspiring and enhancing the diverse communities of the greater Houston area through the power of Music Arts.

Inspire | Nuture | Enhance

Amicis Cor Musica, Inc., formerly known as Co-Cathedral Music of Greater Houston is a not-for-profit organization designed to serve the Greater Houston music arts community. The community includes all levels of musicians from professional to beginners. The organization was founded by the music department of the Co Cathedral of the Sacred Heart located in downtown Houston, Texas. However, it is a separate and independent organization from the church.

2022-2025 Objectives

  • Provide performance opportunities to professional musidians.
  • Develop opportunities for amateur musicians.
  • Provide emerging musicians with skills to propel theri careers.
  • Provide children of the Greater Houston community with educational music experiences.
